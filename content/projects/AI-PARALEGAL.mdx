AI Paralegal is an AI-powered legal assistant that leverages Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and advanced natural language processing to provide legal assistance efficiently.

- Built using LangChain, TogetherAI API, and AWS
- Supports complex legal queries with high accuracy
- Optimized for real-time legal document retrieval
