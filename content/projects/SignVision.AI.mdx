SignVision.AI is an intelligent sign language interpretation system leveraging deep learning and computer vision techniques to provide real-time translation of sign language into text and speech.

- Built using Python, OpenCV, MediaPipe, AWS Lambda, and Google Text-to-Speech (gTTS)
- Achieves 92% accuracy in ASL letter recognition and 85% accuracy in word recognition
- Optimized for real-time interaction with a latency of under 100ms
